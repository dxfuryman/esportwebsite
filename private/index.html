About Us

Frolic esports was established on September 18 2019, with the goal to bring Indian esports scene up to the rise. Frolic esports provide the platform for the gamers to showcase their skills and nurture their talents. Gaming is still been criticised in India. We hope to change that and encourage youth to come and play with us
